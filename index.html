Meet Yael

Your Personal Guide to Israeli Real Estate

I'm not here to sell you a specific property. I'm here to help you find the right one.

Buying property in Israel can be overwhelming — especially from abroad. Different laws, unfamiliar neighborhoods, language barriers, and a maze of bureaucracy. That's where I come in.

With over 30 apartments sold, I've built deep expertise and a trusted network of lawyers, mortgage brokers, contractors, and inspectors. I work exclusively for you — understanding what you need, what you can afford, and what will serve you best long-term.
